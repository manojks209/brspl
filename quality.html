Precision Through Process

BRSPL's reputation is built on uncompromising batch-to-batch consistency. Our Quality Assurance program governs every step, from raw material sourcing to final firing tests.

Advanced Laboratory & Testing Facilities

Particle Size Analysis

Crucial for pigment performance, our analyzers ensure optimal micronization for uniform dispersion in glazes and bodies, minimizing specking.

X-Ray Diffraction (XRD)

Used for phase identification and quantitative analysis, the XRD confirms the formation and purity of the spinel or colorant crystal structure.

Colorimeter & HRS Testing

All batches are tested using a high-precision colorimeter and a lab-scale High-Speed Roller (HRS) kiln for real-world simulation and strict color matching.

Our 4-Step Quality Control Guarantee

1

Raw Material Verification

Incoming raw materials are subjected to rigorous chemical analysis before acceptance to ensure initial purity and compliance with formulation requirements.

2

Inter-Process Testing

Mid-batch samples are taken after calcination and milling. Checks are performed on fineness, homogeneity, and chemical composition before further processing.

3

Final Batch Approval (Firing)

The finished product is fired in our controlled kiln environment on a standard reference glaze/body to confirm color strength and shade conformity.

4

Packaging & Traceability

Each drum is stamped with a unique batch number, allowing full traceability back to the raw material receipt and the QC firing records for audit purposes.

Driving Innovation in Ceramic Colour

Our R&D department is dedicated to developing the next generation of cost-effective, high-performance, and environmentally sustainable ceramic stains.
