Our Legacy of Excellence

For over three decades, BRSPL has pioneered the ceramic color industry in India, combining indigenous expertise with global technical standards.

BRSPL: Coloring India Since 1982

Established in 1982, BRSPL (formerly a joint venture with a European leader) has grown into India's leading manufacturer of Glaze and Body Stains. Our journey began with a commitment to providing the Indian ceramic industry with internationally benchmarked color quality and consistency.

We operate two state-of-the-art manufacturing plants in Navi Mumbai and Ankleshwar, utilizing advanced German technologies and process controls. Our core strength lies in our ability to deliver highly stable colors for demanding applications, backed by 20 years of continuous technical knowledge sharing.

Our total manufacturing capacity stands at 3,000 tonnes per annum (TPA), serving major clients in Vitrified Tiles, Sanitary Ware, and traditional Pottery sectors.
